Sun Tracking Solar Panel

The Sun Tracking Solar Panel was my sophomore year project which used a simple one axis system to follow the sun through out the day to improve efficiency. The solar panels efficiency increased from 7% to 11% during peak hours.

During my senior year I made an animatronic. This was a real size animatronic that moved its arms in a similar motion to human shoulders. The purpose of this low-budget project learn prototyping in low budgets and improve build skills.

Achievements and Experiences

Mentor in Havard Extension School course

During the summer of 2023 I was a co-mentor in the Harvard Extension School's Intro to Entrepreneurship course where I was chosen because of my passion and knowledge in Entrepreneurship and Engineering. I guided a team of 4 students in creating a business proposal for SolClarity: a solar company based in Boston. I became the youngest mentor in the history of the course. Internship at System Technology Works

I interned at System Technolgy Works during my senior year spring semester. I worked with a team of 3 others. We focused on expanding the abilities of the humanoid robot Zeus 2Q. We also made Zeus 2Q rap, sing, dance, do handshakes, and answer questions asked using Lama AI.
